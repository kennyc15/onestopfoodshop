Recipe Title

Savory Spring Chicken Stack

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Chicken breast
  • Spring onions
  • Red onion
  • Arugula
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon mustard
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  • Preheat the oven to 200 degrees Celsius.
  • Peel and dice the sweet potatoes into small cubes. Toss them with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Spread them on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes until tender and slightly crispy.
  • Season the chicken breast with salt, pepper, and a pinch of paprika. Grill or pan-sear the chicken until fully cooked. Let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it thinly.
  • In a small bowl, mix mayonnaise with Dijon mustard to create a spread for the sandwich.
  • Slice the bread and toast it lightly if desired.
  • To assemble the sandwich, spread the mayonnaise mixture on one side of the bread slices.
  • Layer the sliced chicken on one slice of bread, followed by a handful of arugula, roasted sweet potatoes, thinly sliced red onion, and chopped spring onions.
  • Top with the second slice of bread to create a sandwich.
  • Serve the Savory Spring Chicken Stack with extra roasted sweet potatoes on the side.
  • Enjoy your flavorful and satisfying sandwich!

Notes

  • Feel free to customize the sandwich by adding a slice of cheese or avocado for extra creaminess. You can also drizzle some balsamic glaze for a tangy kick.

Recipe Title

Rainbow Chicken Hash

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Red onion
  • Arugula
  • Spring onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 200 degrees Celsius.
  • Peel and dice the potatoes into small cubes. Place them on a baking tray, drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with paprika, salt, and black pepper. Roast in the oven for 25-30 minutes or until golden and crispy.
  • In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add diced red onion and spring onions, saut√© until softened.
  • Add diced bell peppers and sliced chicken breast to the skillet. Season with garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Cook until the chicken is cooked through.
  • Once the potatoes are roasted, add them to the skillet with the chicken and vegetables. Mix well to combine.
  • Turn off the heat and stir in the arugula until it wilts slightly.
  • Serve the Rainbow Chicken Hash hot, garnished with some extra spring onions.

Notes

  • Feel free to top with a dollop of Greek yogurt or a sprinkle of grated cheese for added creaminess.

Recipe Title

Peppy Protein Power Salad

Ingredients

  • Salad grain
  • Red onion
  • Arugula
  • Bell peppers
  • Chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  • Cook the chicken breast in a skillet with olive oil over medium-high heat until fully cooked. Season with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Once cooked, set aside to cool before slicing into strips.
  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the salad grain, sliced red onion, arugula, and diced bell peppers.
  • In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper to make the dressing.
  • Add the sliced chicken breast strips to the salad bowl and drizzle the dressing over the salad.
  • Toss everything together until the salad is well coated with the dressing and the ingredients are evenly distributed.
  • Serve the Peppy Protein Power Salad in a large bowl and enjoy a flavorful and nutritious meal!

Notes

  • Feel free to customize this salad by adding your favorite nuts or seeds for extra crunch and nutrition. You can also top it with a sprinkle of feta cheese or a dollop of hummus for added flavor.
